Welcome to the New Story Charity World Housing Day Banner CDN

Follow the instructions below to add a banner to your site. Banners are available in 3 sizes.

The banner will automatically appear on your site on October 1-3, 2017 for World Housing Day.

Small

Add the following markup to the HTML page on which you want the banner

Add this style link tag to your head

<link href="https://d1icmcy2lixord.cloudfront.net/new-story-banner.css" rel="stylesheet" type="text/css">

Add this div and script tag to the bottom of your body, before the closing tag

<div id="new-story-overlay-for-october" class="new-story-body"></div>
<script type="text/javascript" src="https://d1icmcy2lixord.cloudfront.net/NewStorySmallBanner.js"></script>
